World Skill Centre (WSC)

The World Skill Center (WSC), is an Advanced Skill Training Institute and Section 8 Company, under the Odisha Skill Development Authority, Govt. of Odisha. Set up in Bhubaneshwar, with technical assistance from ITEES, WSC caters primarily to the best talent from an ITI or a Polytechnic and trains them to become globally employable in emerging areas, known more popularly as “industry 4.0”.

Modelled after ITE, Singapore - the Harvard or MIT of the skill world today - WSC is a state of the art, 18-storey, almost half a million square feet, air-conditioned building in the heart of Bhubaneswar. It has a collection of 8 Centres of Excellence in terms of current curriculum and the hands-on experience available by bringing in global curriculum, pedagogy and super-state-of-the-art equipment. The eight disciplines offered are: Mechatronics, Facility Technology – Mechanical & Electrical Services, Facility Technology – Vertical Transportation, Facility Technology – Air-Conditioning and Refrigeration, Precision Engineering, Electrical Technology. In addition, there are two disciplines in the creative economy category. These are Beauty and Wellness and Hair Fashion and Design. In addition to being a Finishing School of global standard, the WSC also aims to work in a hub and spoke model to upgrade trainer capability in the skilling eco-system and run programmes that extend to existing skill institutions in Odisha.

World Skill Centre (WSC) was inaugurated by Hon’ble Chief Minister of Odisha, Shri Naveen Patnaik on 5th March, 2021. WSC is India’s first and one-of-its-kind premier institute of Skill to impart advanced skills and create globally employable youth in the State. World Skill Centre is being set up with a loan from ADB & technical assistance from ITEES, Singapore.

Unlike anything in India so far, WSC will be a major milestone in establishing ‘Skilled-in-Odisha’ as a global brand. A state-of-the-art glass and steel facility spanning half a million square foot including a helipad, World Skill Centre will offer advanced level training in skills from the manufacturing and service sectors. The edge of getting skilled at the World Skill Centre will be the global industry exposure it would provide. WSC will house a Skill Museum and an advanced facility for training the trainers.

Initially the WSC will run 3 advance skill programme such as:

  • Facility Technology - Mechanical & Electrical Services
  • Facility Technology - Air Conditioning & Refrigeration
  • Mechatronics

Initially 80 students in each of the above courses will be enrolled in the centre for the year 2021-2022. These students can either join post ITI or after their diploma and the have to sit for an entrance test to join these advance programmes. The programmes will be residential and international faculties will be engaged for training.

Not only will this larger-than-life initiative mark Bhubaneswar in the skill map of the world, it will inspire the youth as well as the big players in the global economy get associated with the ‘Skilled-in-Odisha’ brand
